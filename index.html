Our Capabilities

Advanced Manufacturing
Machines & Processes

State-of-the-art equipment operated by certified professionals to deliver consistent, repeatable quality at every scale.

CNC Machining

Multi-axis CNC milling and turning for complex geometries with tolerances down to ±0.005 mm. Suitable for prototypes and high-volume production runs.

  • Tolerance: ±0.005 mm
  • Materials: Steel, Aluminium, Brass
  • Batch: 1 – 10,000 units

Laser Cutting

High-precision fiber laser cutting for sheet metal with ultra-clean edges and minimal heat distortion. Ideal for intricate profiles and large flat patterns.

  • Sheet thickness: Up to 20 mm
  • Cutting speed: High-efficiency
  • Edge finish: Burr-free

Metal Fabrication

End-to-end metal fabrication including bending, welding, and assembly. We handle structural frames, enclosures, and custom assemblies with consistent quality.

  • Welding: MIG, TIG, Spot
  • Bending: CNC press brake
  • Finish: Powder coat, paint

How We Work

Our Manufacturing
Process

01

Design & Engineering

We work closely with your team to review drawings, CAD files, and specifications. Our engineers optimise designs for manufacturability without compromising function.

02

Manufacturing

Parts are produced using our advanced CNC, laser, and fabrication equipment. Skilled operators follow strict process controls to ensure consistency across every unit in the batch.

03

Quality Check

Every component undergoes dimensional inspection using calibrated instruments. We maintain full inspection records and can provide certificates of conformance on request.

04

Delivery

Parts are carefully packaged and dispatched on schedule. We offer flexible delivery options and maintain transparent communication from dispatch to doorstep.
